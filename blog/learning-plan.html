Learning Plan

02/07/2021

My long term goal is to be able to get a job working in the web development / front-end development space, just to get a feel for what this type of work is about and see if it is something I want to pursue moving forward.

My strengths would be my mindset, rational thinking and setting / achieving goals and milestones. My limitations are sometimes setting standards too high, being impatient and overly critical of myself.

I would like to see my leadership skills developed at EDA, and be more confident in being able to communicate my ideas with a group and help to align people towards a common goal.

I will manage my workload in this programme by using the pomodorro technique where I work in 25 minute increments with 5 minute breaks in between. I will manage my time by using timeboxing and tracking the time I spend on different components of the course with Toggl. When I get stuck I will force myself to ask for help as this is something I need to work on. I will ask either the foundations channel on Slack or one of the facillitators if needed when I cannot help myself by first searching Google etc. When things are building up and starting to get to me I will try to let one of the facilitators know and hopefully forumulate with them a way for me to prioritise and chunk out the work ahead of me into a more manageable structure.

I would say what I would expect from the facilitation team would just be to be good listeners and maybe not neccessarily give me answers but more so give me clarity and a direction to move in if I get stuck. I also would expect they could bring a good overarching perspective and help me prioritise parts of the course that are really important over those that are less so. Lastly I would expect that they would be able to give me some insights from their experiences as programmers that might help me gain some perspective that I would not get as somebody just starting out.

Scheduling wise I pretty much have the whole day free, I spend 1 hour at the gym 3 days a week and train / work at a muay thai gym probably 4-5 times a week at around 5-6pm so past this time I would allocate to other things like muay thai, gym, cooking etc.
